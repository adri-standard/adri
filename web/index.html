An Open Standard for Data Readiness

ADRI provides a standardized framework for ensuring AI agents can trust their data. Built by the community, for the community.

Why ADRI Exists

AI engineers worldwide face the same challenge: agents making confident but wrong decisions because they can't assess the quality of their data. ADRI emerged from this shared experience.

As an open standard, ADRI belongs to everyone. We believe data readiness is too important to be proprietary.

The Five Dimensions

  • Freshness: Does the data tell agents when it was created or updated?
  • Validity: Does the data include signals about its structural correctness?
  • Completeness: Can agents distinguish between "missing" and "not applicable"?
  • Consistency: Are related data points compatible with each other?
  • Plausibility: Can agents detect outliers or implausible values?
